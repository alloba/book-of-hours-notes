# Sacra Limiae

## Description
Sacra Limiae

The rites of an order of immortals who have tried to remain apart. These rites touch on secrets of Hours of the [[world-at-large/The Mansus|Mansus]] - chiefly gods-who-were-flesh and gods-from-Light.
