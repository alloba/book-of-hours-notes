# Musgrave's Sketches

## Description
Musgrave's Sketches

[[World/Musgrave Dewulf|Musgrave]], 4th Baron Brancrug, was widely mocked as 'the Bleating Baron' and 'the Lamb Dewulf', but these sketches reveal a complex, sombre inner life.
